Kwek Kwek

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Quail eggs 12 pieces
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Cornstarch 2 tablespoons
Water 3/4 cup
Orange food coloring 1 teaspoon
Cooking oil For frying

Steps

  1. Boil quail eggs for about 5 minutes. Let them cool, then peel off the shells.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, water, and food coloring until smooth to form the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Dip each quail egg into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Carefully drop the coated eggs into the hot oil and fry until crispy and golden.
  6. Remove the fried eggs from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce.
