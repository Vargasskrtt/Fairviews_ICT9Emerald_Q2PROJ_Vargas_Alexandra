Pancit Bihon

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Rice Vermicelli (Bihon) 200g
Pork, thinly sliced 150g
Chicken Breast, thinly sliced 150g
Carrots, julienned 1 medium
Cabbage, shredded 1 cup
Garlic, minced 4 cloves
Onion, sliced 1 large
Soy Sauce 3 tablespoons
Chicken Broth 2 cups
Cooking Oil 2 tablespoons
Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Green Onions, chopped 2 tablespoons

Steps

  1. Soak Bihon noodles in warm water until soft, then drain.
  2. In a pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant. Add pork and chicken, cook until browned.
  3. Add carrots, cabbage, and soy sauce. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Cook until vegetables are tender and flavors meld.
  5. Combine Bihon noodles into the pan and mix well. Season with pepper and adjust taste as needed.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions. Serve hot.
