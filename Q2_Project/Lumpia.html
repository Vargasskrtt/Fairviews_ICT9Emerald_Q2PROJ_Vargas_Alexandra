Lumpia

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
Ground pork 1 pound
Chopped onion ½ cup
Garlic 2 cloves, crushed
Minced carrots ½ cup
Chopped green onions ½ cup
Green cabbage ½ cup thinly sliced
Fresh cilantro (Optional) 2 tablespoons chopped

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet and cook ground pork until browned.
  2. Add onion, garlic, carrots, green onions, cabbage, and cilantro. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Let the filling cool, then wrap in lumpia wrappers.
  4. Fry until golden brown and serve warm with dipping sauce.
