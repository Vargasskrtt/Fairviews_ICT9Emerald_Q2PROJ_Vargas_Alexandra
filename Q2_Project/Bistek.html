Bistek

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Beef Sirloin 500g
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
Lemon or Calamansi Juice 1/4 cup
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Onion 2 large, sliced into rings
Cooking Oil 2 tablespoons
Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Sugar 1 tablespoon (optional)
Water 1 cup

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef sirloin in soy sauce, lemon juice, garlic, pepper, and a pinch of salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the marinated beef until browned on all sides.
  3. Add water to the pan and bring to a simmer. Let the beef cook until tender, usually about 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Once the beef is tender, add sliced onions and cook until they soften.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and sugar if needed. Serve Bistek hot with steamed rice.
