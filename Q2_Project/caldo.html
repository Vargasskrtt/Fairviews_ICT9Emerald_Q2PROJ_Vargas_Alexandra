Chicken Arroz Caldo

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
1 cup glutinous rice 1 cup
Regular rice 1/4 cup
Chicken 1 pound, cut into pieces
Onion 1 small, chopped
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Ginger 1 piece, sliced
Chicken broth 6 cups
Fish sauce 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper To taste
Optional toppings Chopped green onions, fried garlic, calamansi juice

Steps

  1. Rinse the glutinous and regular rice thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Saut√© the garlic, onion, and ginger in a large pot until aromatic.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Stir in the rice and cook briefly to coat with the aromatic mixture.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Lower the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, until the rice is tender and the mixture thickens.
  7. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper as desired.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with your preferred toppings like fried garlic or calamansi juice.
