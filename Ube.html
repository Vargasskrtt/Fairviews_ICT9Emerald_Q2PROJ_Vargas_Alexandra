Ube Macapuno Cake

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Ube yam (purple yam) 1 cup, cooked and mashed
All-purpose flour 2 1/2 cups
Baking powder 3 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Butter 1/2 cup, softened
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Eggs 4 large
Evaporated milk 1 cup
Macapuno (coconut strings) 1 jar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in mashed ube. Gradually add dry ingredients alternating with evaporated milk, starting and ending with dry ingredients.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool cakes completely before frosting and layering with macapuno.
