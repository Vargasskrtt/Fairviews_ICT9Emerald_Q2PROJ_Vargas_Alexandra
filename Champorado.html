Champorado

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Glutinous Rice 1 cup
Water 4 cups
Dark Chocolate 100g
Condensed Milk 1/4 cup
Salt 1/4 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Rinse the glutinous rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, combine rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer until the rice is cooked and softened.
  3. Stir in dark chocolate until fully melted and incorporated.
  4. Add condensed milk and salt. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes over low heat.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy.
