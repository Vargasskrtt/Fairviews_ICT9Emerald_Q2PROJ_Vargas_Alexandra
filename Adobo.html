Famous Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Chicken thighs 2 pounds
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
Vinegar 1/3 cup
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Bay leaves 2
Ground black pepper 1 teaspoon
Water 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and pepper in a bowl. Add chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Brown the chicken on both sides, then remove and set aside.
  3. Pour the marinade into the skillet and bring to a boil. Add water and the browned chicken.
  4. Lower the heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and tender, about 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
